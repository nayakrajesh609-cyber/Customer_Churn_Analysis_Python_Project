# 🛍️ Customer Segmentation & Churn Analysis using Python

# 📌 Project Summary

Developed a Customer Segmentation and Churn Analysis project using Python to analyze customer purchasing behavior, revenue contribution, and retention patterns.
Applied RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis to segment customers into actionable business groups.
Identified high-value customers, churn risks, revenue drivers, and customer loyalty patterns to support customer retention strategies.

# 🎯 Business Objectives

Segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
Identify high-value and loyal customers.
Detect customers at risk of churn.
Analyze revenue contribution across customer groups.
Understand product category and regional performance.
Support customer retention and marketing strategies.
